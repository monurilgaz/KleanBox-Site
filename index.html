How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Three simple steps to a cleaner SMS inbox.

1

Install & Enable

Download KleanBox and follow the guided setup to enable SMS filtering in your iPhone settings.

2

Set Your Rules

Create custom filters or let Smart Filtering do the work with community-driven definitions.

3

Enjoy the Silence

Spam and promotions are filtered away. Important messages stay right where they belong.

🛡

Your messages stay on your device

KleanBox processes everything locally using Apple's SMS filtering framework. No messages are ever sent to external servers. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection. Your privacy isn't a feature — it's the foundation.
