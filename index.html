KleanBox

SMS Spam Blocker for iPhone

KleanBox filters SMS messages from unknown senders, keeping your inbox clean and organized. Create custom rules or let Smart Filtering automatically classify spam, promotions, OTPs, and more.

Features

📝

Custom Rules

Create keyword or regex-based filter rules. Match by message content, sender, or both.

🧠

Smart Filtering

Community-maintained filter definitions automatically classify messages when your rules don't match.

📁

Organized Categories

Messages are sorted into Spam, Promotions, OTP, and Transactions automatically.

🔒

Privacy First

Everything runs locally on your device. Your messages never leave your phone.

⚙️

Full Control

Toggle categories, enable or disable rules, and preview filters before saving.

🚀

Easy Setup

Guided onboarding walks you through enabling the filter in just a few taps.
